What is Morse Code?

Morse code has been used since the 1800s in the invention of the telegram which had made means of communication much quicker than the postal system. Morse code uses a series of clicks and beeps to create an alphabet of letters. For example, a dash or a beep would represent the letter I. Morsecode is used today as a means of communication amongst the military and generally anyone who wants to use it. Enjoy!
